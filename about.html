About Me

Hi there, I'm an aspiring web developer (already got the 'stache).

A few years ago, I started my career in Finance. Working for one of the biggest banking group in the world felt pretty good until I had enough of the bureaucratic limitations. Something had to change.

So after a couple of months back in Europe with my family, I realize what I desired most: freedom.

Freedom of working wherever I wanted, whenever I wanted. Coding allows me to do so. No more 8 to 5 gig, 5 days a week. I did it kind of suddenly with absolutely no previous coding eperience, and I hope it will inspire other people to do the same.
